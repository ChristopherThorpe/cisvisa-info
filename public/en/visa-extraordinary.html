Extraordinary abilities

These visas are for prominent or highly-skilled people. They cover artists, athletes, entertainers, fashion models, some businesspeople. You must be coming to the US for an event or a job that you've already arranged.

Examples
  • Scientist with published research and awards
  • Sports team attending an elite-level competition
  • Well-known movie or TV show actor
  • Technical expert who earns world-class fees for their professional work
Key Facts
  • You must be unusually good at what you do and be able to prove it. You should be winning awards, being mentioned in the press, or be internationally-known within your field.
  • Some of these visas are tied to a specific event or performance. These allow you to bring others who are an essential part of your event. A musician, for instance, could bring band members and their manager.
  • Other types of these visas are for taking a job in the USA, with an organization that's ready to sponsor you. These can lead to a green card, and they allow you to bring family members.
