Privacy Policy

Protecting your private information is our priority. This Privacy Policy applies to the CISVisa.com website and governs data collection and usage. By using the CISVisa.com website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Consolidated Information Services, LLC ("CIS") owns and operates CISVisa.com. CIS may collect personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. If you purchase CIS products and services, we collect and store billing and credit card information.

We do not sell, rent or lease our customer lists to third parties.

CIS collects and uses your personal information to operate the CISVisa.com website and deliver the services you have requested. CIS will only share your information with third parties who provide essential components of its service, such as credit card processing or package delivery. We will only disclose your information to other parties if, in our judgment, we are required to do so by law or to comply with legal processes.

CIS will send communications, such as a welcome email, as part of these services. CIS may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from CIS and its affiliates, or to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Information about your computer hardware and software may also be automatically collected. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used by CIS for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the CISVisa.com website.

The CISVisa.com website may use "cookies" to make the service work. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. You can decline to accept cookies, but if you do so we may be unable to provide the service to you.

CIS keeps track of visitors’ web browsing behavior within CISVisa.com. This data is used to improve the service and to deliver customized content and advertising.

CIS may also aggregate and publish anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your city, country, age, gender, preferences and interests.

CIS reserves the right to delete customer information at any time.

CIS does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen. If you are under the age of thirteen, you must ask your parent or guardian for permission to use this website.

CIS will occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect company and customer feedback. CIS encourages you to periodically review this Policy to be informed of how CIS is protecting your information.

We welcome your comments regarding this Privacy Policy. If you believe that CISVisa.com has not adhered to this Policy, please contact us at privacy@cisvisa.com.
