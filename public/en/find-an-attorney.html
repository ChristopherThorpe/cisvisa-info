How do I find a good attorney?

We look for several things in an attorney. If your friends or family have worked with someone in the past, and it's gone well, that's a good place to start.

  • They should have significant experience, in different types of cases, since odd things can happen during a visa application and (despite some advertisements) no one can make guarantees.
  • They should speak your language, so that you can communicate clearly with them.
  • And finally, they should make you feel comfortable.
We can connect you with an attorney we personally know and trust. There is no obligation, and it's your decision whether to hire them. See below for more details.
