How do I find a good attorney?

We look for several things in an attorney. If your friends or family can refer you, that's a good place to start, but otherwise:

  • A good attorney will have experience in different types of cases, since odd things can happen during a visa application and (despite some advertisements) no one can make guarantees.
  • Attorneys should speak your language, so that you can communicate clearly with them.
  • And finally, your attorney should make you feel comfortable.
