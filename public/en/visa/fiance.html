Fiancé(e) Visas

This visa is for people who plan to come to the USA to immediately marry a US citizen.

Key Facts:


  • This is a very specific type of visa. It gives you 90 days to come to the USA, get married, and (now that you're a spouse rather than a fiancé) apply for permanent residency. There isn't much flexibility here. If you take longer than 90 days for the wedding, for instance, you'll be deported.
  • In some cases, you may want to consider getting married first, or coming to the US on another visa type then applying for an adjustment of status. These are complicated decisions, so you'll want to get an attorney's advice here.
  • The official requirement is that you must have seen each other, in person, within the last 2 years. Consular officials, however, often ask additional questions in an interview to make sure the relationship is genuine, and that the US citizen can financially support his/her fiance.
  • This visa can allow you to work soon after you arrive, and to bring your unmarried children under 21 years of age.
