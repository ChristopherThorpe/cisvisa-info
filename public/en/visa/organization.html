Sponsoring visas

Organizations bring foreign workers to the USA for many reasons. The right one for you depends on which type of organization you represent.

Farm (seasonal work)
  • Allows agricultural firms to hire foreign workers for temporary or seasonal work.
  • Special program with specific administrative rules.
  • Single petition to USCIS covers multiple workers.
  • Visa type: H-2A
Other seasonal work
  • Often used by hospitality industry for summer (beach) or winter (skiing) destinations
  • Some limits on eligible countries
  • Visa type: H-2B
Church
  • For employees of well-established religions organizations.
  • Intense review process, including site visits, due to history of fraudulent applications.
  • Visa type: R-1, R-2
Transportation company
  • D visas are for the crew of airplanes, boats, or other vehicles stopping in the USA. C visas are for persons exclusively in transit to other countries. A C-1/D combination is possible.
  • Consult the reciprocity tables for D and C visa eligibility.
  • Visa types: D, C-1
