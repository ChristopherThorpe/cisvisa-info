Media and journalists

This is the only visa that lets you report news from the USA. If you're coming on behalf of a foreign media outlet, this is also the only visa you're eligible for.

Examples
  • Newspaper or magazine reporters
  • Film crews for a TV show
Key Facts
  • This visa covers factual reporting, not art. If you plan to take artistic photographs or paint pictures, for example, you'd need to use a different visa type.
  • You must be representing a recognized media outlet. This can be a newspaper, magazine, radio / television show, or a documentary film.
  • You can bring your spouse and family with you.
