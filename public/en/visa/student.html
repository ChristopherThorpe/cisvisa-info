Study in the USA

Visitors who intend to come to the United States to study must obtain a student visa.

Key Facts

  • If you have a visitor visa, you may study less than 18 hours per week without credit.
  • Your visa cannot be issued more than 120 days before you register for your studies.
  • You may not enter the US more than 30 days on your student visa before before you register for your studies. You may enter on other visas.
  • If you are in the United States, you must apply to "adjust your status" to a student visa before your studies begin.
  • You have limited options to work on a student visa.
  • You must show evidence of sufficient funds to pursue your proposed course of study.
  • You must intend to depart the US when your studies are complete.
