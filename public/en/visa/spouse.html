Spouse and children

US citizens and permanent residents can sponsor their spouses and children to come live with them in the USA.
⇒Neither my spouse nor I is a citizen or permanent resident...

Key Facts:


  • This visa covers spouses and unmarried children under the age of 21.
  • The sponsor must show that they can financially support people they are sponsoring. Learn more about the Affidavit of Support.
  • Your spouse may be able to join you in the USA while your application is being processed. This is done with a K-3 visa.
  • The time it takes to get this visa depends on the sponsor's status (citizen or permanent resident) and the country where their family members currently lives.
  • Your attorney may file multiple applications at the same time to get you into the US as quickly as possible.
